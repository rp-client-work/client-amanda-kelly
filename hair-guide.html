What is Virgin Hair?

Our Virgin hair is chemically unprocessed, harvested from a single donor. It remains in its natural state, ensuring the cuticle is intact and aligned. This is the gold standard of luxury hair extensions—durable, dyeable, and undeniably authentic.

Closure vs. Frontal

Choosing the right finishing piece is essential for a seamless, natural-looking hairline. Both offer HD Transparent lace for maximum melt.

4x4 Closure

  • Low Maintenance
  • Perfect for Beginners
  • Fixed Parting Area
  • Protecting natural hairline

13x4 Frontal

  • High Versatility
  • Ear-to-Ear Coverage
  • Multiple Parting Options
  • Allows for Updos/Ponytails

Pro Tip:

"If you're active or new to extensions, go with a closure. If you're a styling pro seeking a truly custom scalp illusion, the frontal is your weapon of choice."
